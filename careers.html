Lady of the House: General Manager

Job Description

We are looking for a General Manager based in the UK. This role is a key senior leadership position with the opportunity to progress as the company grows.

We are looking for someone with a passion for upholding our mission to Celebrate, Champion and Honour women in the Dance music industry. You will be responsible for leading and building a team of dedicated specialists providing bespoke, creative solutions to help all women thrive in the electronic music industry.

You will lead on all internal projects and actively work to develop new business leads by working with existing and new stakeholders and partners.

Your role is focused on the six key tasks; shaping the work environment, setting short-mid term strategy based on longer-term goals, allocating resources, developing team members and managers, building the organisation, and overseeing operations.

As an ideal candidate, you will possess a passion for women in electronic music and serving the communities within this.

As a person, you will need to be engaging, and reliable, with good communication skills, enjoy partnership working and be very resourceful.

You will be responsible for improving efficiency and increasing departmental profits while managing the company's overall operations

  • Directly reporting to the advisory board

  • Oversee, motivate and encourage team, specialists and contractors
  • Hire, train, and evaluate new employees, specialists and contractors
  • Lead on client projects ensuring that they are accurately quoted, the right resources are allocated, and that the fulfilled to the highest standards, on time and within budget
  • Lead on marketing & PR working alongside our external teams
  • Lead & build on relationships with stakeholders such as Artists, Venues, Festivals, Partners
